## 😐 NEUTRAL / NEUTRE

If no positive or negative words are detected, the emoji will be 😐 (neutral).

## 🧪 Test Examples - Try These!

### 😊 POSITIVE Examples:
- "This course was **amazing** and **great**!" → 😊
- "The instructor is **fantastic** and **professional**!" → 😊
- "**Excellent** content, **highly recommend**!" → 😊
- "**Wonderful** learning experience, **well done**!" → 😊
- "The lessons were **helpful** and **informative**!" → 😊

### 😞 NEGATIVE Examples:
- "The content was **terrible** and **boring**" → 😞
- "**Worst** course ever, **waste of time**!" → 😞
- "**Disappointed**, the instructor is **unprofessional**" → 😞
- "**Horrible** experience, **avoid** this course!" → 😞
- "**Useless** content, **confusing** and **messy**" → 😞

### 😐 NEUTRAL Examples:
- "The course was **okay**, nothing special" → 😐
- "Average content, could be better" → 😐
- "It's fine, not bad but not great either" → 😐

---

## 🔄 How it works / Fonctionnement

1. The system counts **positive** and **negative** words in your comment
2. If positive words > negative words → 😊 (POSITIF)
3. If negative words > positive words → 😞 (NÉGATIF)
4. If equal or no keywords → 😐 (NEUTRE)

**Note:** The rating (1-5 stars) also influences the result:
- Rating ≥ 4 → Likely POSITIF (unless many negative words)
- Rating ≤ 2 → Likely NEGATIF (unless many positive words)
- Rating = 3 → Based on keywords only
